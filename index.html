Smalltalk Speech

Child-centered speech & feeding therapy in your home

Our Services

We offer in-home services in Bentonville, Bella Vista, Centerton, and Pea Ridge, and telehealth services throughout Arkansas.

Evaluations

Evaluations are the first step in getting to know your child. Through play, family input, and standardized assessment, we assess your child’s strengths and areas to support. Afterward, we’ll create an individualized plan of care, share our findings, and discuss next steps to support your child’s growth.

Speech Therapy

In speech therapy, we use child-directed play and structured activities to help your child build communication skills. Sessions are personalized to your child’s needs. We’ll partner with you to practice developing skills at home and support lasting progress across settings.

Feeding Therapy

In feeding therapy, we help your child enjoy mealtimes and feel more comfortable with eating. We focus on building oral motor skills, expanding food repertoire, and building positive mealtime routines. We encourage family involvement in feeding therapy to facilitate lasting progress across all contexts.

Please note that we are considered an out-of-network provider with all insurances. We can help you seek reimbursement through your out-of-network benefits.

Gina Mortensen

M.A., CCC-SLP, CLC

Gina is a bilingual (Spanish-English) speech-language pathologist. She has more than 6 years of experience supporting children with communication and feeding disorders. Gina has specialized training in the SOS Approach to Feeding, Language Acquisition through Motor Planning (LAMP), Beckman Oral Motor Assessment & Intervention, Hanen More Than Words, and Dynamic Temporal and Tactile Cueing (DTTC). She holds a certificate in Early Intervention through Georgetown University and is a Certified Lactation Counselor (CLC).
